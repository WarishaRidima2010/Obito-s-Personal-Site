I started the 4th Great Ninja War. I got crushed by a rock when I was 13. My Sensei was Namikaze Minato aka 4th Hokage. My teamates were Kakashi Hatake and Rin Nohara. Rin died by Kakashi's chidori. I killed my Sensei. Kakashi was now left alone. I fought him in the ninja war and later became beties again! (I died in war)
